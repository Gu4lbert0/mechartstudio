Woolly

A modular mobile robotic platform designed to transport heavy industrial materials and support multiple tasks through interchangeable attachments and configurable drive systems.

Back to Projects

Project Summary

The Woolly project focused on developing a modular industrial mobile platform for use in factory environments. Designed to transport large plastic rods and other heavy materials, the platform supports interchangeable attachments that allow it to perform a variety of material-handling tasks. Multiple drive system options were developed to enable operation in different environments, ranging from smooth factory floors to more demanding terrain. Additional information about the platform can be found on www.woollyindustrial.com.

My Role

  • Contributed to the mechanical design of the drive system.
  • Helped with designing and manufacturing fixtures used during assembly.
  • Performed mechanical assembly and system integration.
  • Created engineering documentation and bills of materials (BOMs).
  • Machined custom components using a manual lathe.
  • Collaborated with the electrical engineer on the electrical harnessing, electrical system, and safety features.
  • Tested sensors and actuators using CAN bus communication.
