Woolly

Mechanical design and prototype support project involving drive-system work, fixturing, assembly, documentation, machining, harnessing, and testing.

Back to Projects

Project Summary

Woolly involved hands-on engineering support across mechanical design, assembly, documentation, machining, electrical collaboration, and system testing. The work required moving between design tasks and shop-floor execution to help keep prototype development practical and testable.

The project included drive-system mechanical design, fixture design, assembly work, BOM documentation, manual lathe machining, harnessing collaboration, safety feature support, and actuator and sensor testing over CAN bus protocols.

My Role

  • Supported mechanical design for the drive system.
  • Designed fixtures and performed assembly work.
  • Created documentation including bill of materials information.
  • Performed machining work on a manual lathe.
  • Collaborated on harnessing, electrical systems, and safety features.
  • Tested actuators and sensors using CAN bus protocols.
