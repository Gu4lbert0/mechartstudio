Autonomous Drone Platform

Custom UAV platform for mapping, sensing, and field data collection.

Back to Projects

Project Summary

The Autonomous Drone Platform is a custom UAV concept for mapping, sensing, and field data collection. The project centers on combining airframe packaging, payload planning, sensing requirements, and practical deployment needs for agricultural and research environments.

The platform is intended to support field inspection, data capture, and repeatable flight-based sensing workflows where mechanical layout, serviceability, and payload integration are important design constraints.

My Role

  • Supported platform concept development and mechanical integration planning.
  • Evaluated payload, sensing, and mounting considerations for field data collection.
  • Contributed to prototype-focused decisions for manufacturability and serviceability.
  • Helped connect mechanical design requirements with sensing and autonomous system goals.
