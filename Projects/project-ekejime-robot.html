Ekejime Robot

An automated fish-processing system developed to perform the ikejime process through integrated pneumatic and electrical subsystems while operating in a wet industrial environment.

Back to Projects

Project Summary

The Ikejime Robot project focused on the development of an automated system designed to perform the ikejime process used in fish processing. My primary responsibilities included designing the complete pneumatic system, developing the plumbing layout, implementing system safety features, and creating the waterproof 120 VAC electrical harness. The project required careful integration of pneumatic and electrical components to ensure reliable operation in a demanding processing environment.

My Role

  • Designed the complete pneumatic system.
  • Developed the pneumatic plumbing layout.
  • Designed and implemented system safety features.
  • Created the waterproof 120 VAC electrical harness.
  • Integrated pneumatic and electrical subsystems.
  • Supported system assembly and validation.
