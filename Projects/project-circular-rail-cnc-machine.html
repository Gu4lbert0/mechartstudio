Circular Rail CNC Machine

Novel CNC architecture utilizing a circular rail and rotating spindle system.

Back to Projects

Project Summary

The Circular Rail CNC Machine explores a nontraditional CNC architecture built around a circular rail and rotating spindle system. The concept focuses on how rotary motion, structural stiffness, tool positioning, and manufacturable mechanical geometry can be combined into a novel machine layout.

The project emphasizes early-stage machine architecture, motion-system packaging, prototype development, and the practical constraints required to move a unique CNC concept toward physical validation.

My Role

  • Developed mechanical design concepts for circular rail based machine architecture.
  • Considered spindle placement, rotating motion, structure, and manufacturability.
  • Supported CAD modeling and prototype planning for a novel machine layout.
  • Focused on practical constraints needed to test and refine the CNC concept.
