Gather

A modular collaborative robot designed to autonomously transport crops, materials, and equipment while assisting farmworkers in specialty crop operations.

Back to Projects

Project Summary

The Gather project focused on developing a modular collaborative robot designed to autonomously transport crops and materials through vineyards, orchards, nurseries, and other specialty crop operations. The platform was engineered to be compact, easy to operate, and adaptable through interchangeable attachments for a variety of agricultural tasks. Additional information about the platform can be found on Gather.ag.

My Role

  • Used Onshape to design the sheet-metal body and mechanical components of the robotic system, developing CAD models that supported fabrication, assembly, and system integration.
  • Applied engineering analysis and physics principles to evaluate design concepts and ensure the mechanical system met performance requirements.
  • Worked with the electrical engineer to develop and integrate a fully functional electrical system for the robotic platform.
  • Worked closely with electrical, software, and manufacturing engineers to integrate mechanical, electrical, and software subsystems into a functional robotic platform.
  • Developed assembly procedures and manufacturing strategies with the manufacturing team to facilitate efficient construction of the prototype.
  • Collaborated with the software engineer during system testing and validation to support prototype integration and evaluate overall system performance.
  • Developed engineering documentation, including CAD drawings, bills of materials (BOMs), assembly procedures, and technical reports.
  • Participated in investor demonstrations and technical presentations.
