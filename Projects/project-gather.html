Gather

Robotic platform project involving sheet metal body design, mechanical systems, electrical collaboration, assembly, welding, calculations, testing, and investor demonstration support.

Back to Projects

Project Summary

Gather was a robotic platform project that required mechanical design, fabrication awareness, electrical collaboration, testing, documentation, and demonstration readiness. The work centered on creating a functional machine body and mechanical system that could support real prototype operation.

The project required engineering calculations, hands-on assembly and welding involvement, and communication support during investor-facing demonstrations.

My Role

  • Designed the sheet metal body and mechanical system.
  • Collaborated on the electrical system.
  • Supported assembly and welding of the system.
  • Performed required calculations for the design.
  • Handled testing, documentation, and investor demo support.
