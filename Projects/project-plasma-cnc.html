Project Summary

The Plasma CNC project focused on preparing and cutting sheet metal parts using computer-controlled plasma cutting workflows. The work connected CAD geometry, toolpath setup, material preparation, and fabricated parts for prototype or shop use.

The project emphasized practical fabrication constraints, clean digital files, cut quality, and turning design intent into usable physical components.

My Role

  • Prepared CAD profiles and cut files for CNC plasma work.
  • Supported toolpath setup and material planning.
  • Produced sheet metal parts for prototypes and fabrication tasks.
  • Reviewed cut parts and adjusted files for practical manufacturing needs.
