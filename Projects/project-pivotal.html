Project Summary

Pivotal involved providing mechanical design support during project transition and development. The work required understanding existing design intent, helping identify mechanical paths forward, and supporting another engineer through technical decisions.

The project emphasized clear communication, practical mechanical judgment, and continuity across design work so development could keep moving.

My Role

  • Supported project continuity during engineering transition.
  • Reviewed mechanical concepts and helped identify practical design paths.
  • Provided technical support where the project required additional mechanical engineering input.
