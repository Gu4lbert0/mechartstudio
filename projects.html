Portfolio

Representative work spanning field robotics, computer vision, UAVs, and novel mechanical systems.

Engineering work and projects

Architectural 3D Modeling

Architectural 3D modeling project focused on converting blueprint specifications into detailed digital models using Autodesk Inventor. The project supported architectural visualization through both digital deliverables and physical 3D-printed prototypes.

Auto-Header Height Control System

Field engineering support project focused on the installation, operation, troubleshooting, and optimization of automated header height control systems used on commercial harvesting equipment throughout California's agricultural industry.

Apple Harvester

Robotic harvesting system designed to pick, and collect apples while minimizing fruit damage and reducing labor requirements in orchard operations

Module Sortation

Automated industrial sorting system designed to transport, inspect, and process plastic modules through a series of mechanically, electrically, and pneumatically controlled operations.

Woolly

A modular mobile robotic platform designed to transport heavy industrial materials and support multiple tasks through interchangeable attachments and configurable drive systems.

Ekejime Robot

An automated fish-processing system developed to perform the ikejime process through integrated pneumatic and electrical subsystems while operating in a wet industrial environment.

Gather

A modular collaborative robot designed to autonomously transport crops, materials, and equipment while assisting farmworkers in specialty crop operations.

Quadcopters

UAV platform work involving mechanical layout, component integration, wiring, testing, and flight-ready assembly.

Machining

Hands-on fabrication and machining work supporting prototype development, fixtures, components, and mechanical assemblies.

Photogrammetry

Image-based 3D reconstruction work for capturing real-world geometry, documenting spaces, and producing usable visual reference models.

Plasma CNC

Computer-controlled plasma cutting work focused on digital fabrication, toolpath preparation, sheet metal parts, and prototype production.

Object Counting and Mapping

A handheld robotic vision system designed to detect, count, and generate three-dimensional maps of objects by combining computer vision, depth sensing, visual-inertial odometry, and embedded computing into a single portable platform.
